Definition of my name

The feminine name Anushka in Sanskrit is used as a term of endearment, meaning "ray of light" or "lightning". However, it is actually a variant of Annushka, which is a Russian diminutive of Anna. Anna is a version of the name Hannah, which was used in Latin and Greek back in the Old Testament. The name Hannah is taken from Hebrew and means "grace" and "favour".
